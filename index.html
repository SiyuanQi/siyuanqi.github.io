About Me

I am a second year Ph.D. student in the Computer Science Department at University of California, Los Angeles. I am currently doing computer vision research in the Center for Vision, Cognition, Learning, and Autonomy advised by Professor Song-Chun Zhu.

My research interests include Computer Vision, Artificial Intelligence, and Machine Learning.
